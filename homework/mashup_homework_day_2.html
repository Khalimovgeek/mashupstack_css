amg one

The Mercedes-AMG One is a Formula 1 inspired hypercar that brings actual F1 hybrid technology to the road, using a 1.6-liter turbocharged V6 engine derived from Mercedes' championship-winning race car. It produces over 1,000 horsepower through a complex hybrid system with multiple electric motors driving all four wheels. Built in extremely limited numbers, the AMG One represents one of the most advanced and exclusive performance cars ever made.
